Initiative — Problem It Solves

Value exists. The system to prove it doesn't.

Keedian creates measurable customer value every day — but that value is not consistently defined, calculated, communicated, or accessible to customers.

The core problem

Keedian creates measurable customer value through alarm monitoring, site management, dispatch prevention, and operational support. However, this value is not consistently defined, calculated, communicated, or accessible to customers.

Current challenges
  • Manual reporting and MBR preparation
  • Inconsistent KPI definitions across customers
  • Operational knowledge trapped in SOPs and tribal expertise
  • Limited customer visibility into outcomes
  • Data gaps preventing scalable reporting
Customer impact

Customers receive periodic snapshots of value that require significant manual preparation. They lack continuous visibility into the outcomes Keedian is delivering on their behalf.

Internal impact

Teams spend significant time gathering data and preparing reports. Business rules and value definitions live in individual expertise — not in a system — creating inconsistency at scale.

Proposed Solution

Define. Prototype. Hand off the blueprint.

This team's job is not to build the final platform. Leadership was clear: define what the customer needs to see, create the prototype, identify the gaps, and provide the requirements. Leandro's team and the platform roadmap build the long-term solution.

✓ Scope confirmed by Roi & Martino
What changes operationally

A Customer Value Intelligence framework — standardized across the org

Create a consistent method for defining, measuring, calculating, and communicating customer value. Replace tribal knowledge and manual effort with a repeatable system.

What process improves

From data collection → report creation → to continuous visibility

The process shifts from periodic, manual reporting to a calculated, always-available view of customer outcomes — built on standardized definitions and unified data.

What friction is removed

Five sources of operational drag eliminated

  • Manual customer reporting
  • Inconsistent value calculations
  • Multiple data sources with conflicting metrics
  • Limited customer visibility into outcomes
  • Dependence on tribal knowledge for value measurement
What customer outcome changes

Customers see the value Keedian delivers — continuously

Instead of asking what Keedian delivered this month, customers gain real-time visibility into measurable business outcomes — improving transparency, trust, and engagement.

AI Component

Operational Intelligence Layer — not a decision maker

AI assists the framework. It does not make customer commitments, replace Customer Success, or tell Operations what to do.

Capability 01

Automated Value Summarization

Translate operational activities into customer-facing value summaries without manual effort.

Alarms processed Offline sites reduced Dispatches prevented Savings generated
Capability 02

Anomaly Detection

Identify unusual operational behavior before it becomes a customer issue.

Increasing offline locations Abnormal alarm volumes Performance degradation
Capability 03

Predictive Escalation

Detect risks before they become customer issues — proactive signals instead of reactive responses.

Recurring patterns Site instability Escalating alarm trends
Capability 04

Interactive Customer Questioning

Enable customers to ask natural-language questions about their operational performance and receive data-backed answers.

Operational insights Customer self-service Value Q&A
AI provides insights, not commitments. Recommendations remain internal. Customers receive visibility. Keedian receives intelligence.
